The Idea Behind it

 The Palladium Security Minor Hockey Fund was created to help minor hockey organizations develop and roster minor hockey players that may not have the chance to play the game we all love.  


There are always challenges when it comes to raising money for minor hockey in our comunities.  From house legue to AAA Rep hockey, The cost of the sport can set a team almost $80 000 or more for one season.  These funds are paid by parents through the league and team fees.  


These fees can start at the house leauge level from $500 a season, AAA Rep Parents can expect to pay as much as $5000.00 a season. This does not include equipment and travel costs 


This program works with both the organization and or the individual hockey team.
